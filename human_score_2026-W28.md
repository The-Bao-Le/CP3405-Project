## Override Paragraph

The Human Score is more constructive than previous weeks. Macro conditions remain supportive as major U.S. equity indices continued to rise, volatility declined, and a Federal Reserve rate hold remains the most likely outcome. Technical conditions also remain positive, with SPX and IWM maintaining Bullish trends above their key moving averages and NDX continuing its recovery. July seasonality further improves the outlook compared with the weaker conditions experienced throughout June. Nevertheless, rising Treasury yields, higher oil prices, and increasingly hawkish Federal Reserve expectations continue to present important risks. Overall, the balance of evidence supports a Neutral-Bullish outlook with stronger conviction than Week 25.
