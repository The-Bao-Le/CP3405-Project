# Sprint 7 Goal — W29

Deliver a reliable and dependency-controlled W29 forecasting pipeline covering SPX, NDX, IWM, and all 11 S&P 500 sector ETFs.

The team should automate as much of the workflow as possible. Where full automation cannot be achieved, each responsible role must implement partial automation where feasible and manually complete the remaining work before the required deadline. Technical limitations or automation failures must not result in missing deliverables.

R2 must conduct the mandatory midweek check-in by Wednesday to confirm that every role has started its work, identify blockers, and take appropriate action before they become end-of-Sprint problems.

R3, R4, R5, and R6 must complete and submit their assigned deliverables by **Saturday, 18 July 2026 at 2:00 PM SGT**. Their outputs must be available before dependent roles complete the remaining forecasting, synthesis, scoring, calibration, merging, and release activities.

All remaining roles must complete their assigned work by **Sunday, 19 July 2026 at 11:59 PM SGT**. This includes:

- R8 multi-LLM API synthesis and comparison;
- R7 Human Score Wild Card review;
- R10 W28 calibration and cumulative accuracy update;
- R9 branch merging and publication of the correctly formatted `vW29` release tag;
- R2 Definition of Done verification and retrospective evidence.

Sprint success means that blockers are identified and acted on early, the pipeline is automated as far as reasonably possible, all incomplete automated processes are supported by timely manual completion, and every required deliverable is submitted before its assigned deadline.
