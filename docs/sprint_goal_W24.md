# Sprint Goal 2026 - W24

Our Sprint 4 goal is to automate one market evidence collection step using a Python/yfinance script. The script fetches Yahoo Finance data for SPY, QQQ, IWM, XLK, XLE, and XLF, then generates structured CSV/JSON files containing the Friday close market snapshot and last five trading-day closes.

The automated output supports our W4 prediction for SPX, NDX, IWM, Information Technology, Energy, and Financials.
