The strongest agreement this week comes from the LLM comparison and the technical evidence. All four AI models give a Down direction, and the technical agent also reports that SPX, NDX, and IWM are below their 8-day and 21-day EMAs.

## Team Learning
The main learning this week is that strong AI agreement is useful, but it should not be treated as the final answer by itself. The team still needs to compare AI consensus with Human Score, technical signals, macro risks, and almanac evidence.
