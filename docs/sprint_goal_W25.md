Sprint Goal — W25

Automate two forecasting agents, Almanac and Technical, so the team can generate structured market evidence automatically for the W25 prediction. The forecast will cover the three required indices — SPX, NDX, and IWM — plus five S&P sectors: Technology, Energy, Financials, Utilities, and Materials. The sprint will also automate the calibration process by comparing W25 predictions against actual market outcomes using the existing W22 scoring rule for consistency.
