# Sprint Goal - Week 2

This sprint, our goal is to produce a disciplined market prediction by combining seasonal, macro, technical, multi-LLM, and human judgment evidence. The main question we are trying to answer is whether the market can continue upward momentum despite elevated yields and the binary risk from PCE inflation. As our first try at the project, this week priority is not to be perfectly correct, but to evaluate both individual skills and collaboration skills of each member.
