Final Weekly Call
The market is currently constrained by a strong alignment of technical bearishness and unfavorable midterm-year seasonality. While the Technology sector's seasonal strength and potential earnings surprises provide some defense, they are currently outweighed by rising Treasury yields and the technical breakdown below key EMAs. Until the S&P 500 can recapture the 8-day and 21-day EMA levels, the path of least resistance remains to the downside, with the upcoming CPI report serving as the critical juncture for volatility.
